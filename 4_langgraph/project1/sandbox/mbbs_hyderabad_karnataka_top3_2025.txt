Top 3 MBBS colleges in Hyderabad/Karnataka (practical shortlist for 2025)

Note: 2025 cutoffs can vary a lot by quota (AIQ, state, management, minority, NRI) and counseling round. Figures below are indicative from recent 2025/near-2025 public counselling/result pages surfaced in search results.

1) Osmania Medical College, Hyderabad
- Ranking: Among the strongest government medical colleges in Hyderabad; frequently listed as a top choice in Telangana/Hyderabad MBBS admissions.
- 2025 cutoff (General category): around 580+ marks in recent 2025 round patterns surfaced in search results; some sources cite higher expected closing scores for top government colleges.
- Fees: Government college; usually very low compared with private colleges.
- Review signal: Strong reputation, high patient load, strong clinical exposure, highly preferred by NEET candidates.

2) Gandhi Medical College, Secunderabad (Hyderabad region)
- Ranking: Top-tier government medical college in Hyderabad; consistently among the most sought-after MBBS options in Telangana.
- 2025 cutoff (General category): around 590+ marks in recent 2025 patterns surfaced in search results.
- Fees: Government college; low annual tuition compared with private institutions.
- Review signal: Excellent clinical exposure and brand value; highly competitive admission.

3) St. John’s Medical College, Bengaluru, Karnataka
- Ranking: Widely regarded as one of the best private/mission medical colleges in Karnataka; strong academics and hospital training.
- 2025 cutoff (General category): around 550–620 marks depending on quota/round; search snippets indicate the college attracts high-scoring candidates.
- Fees: Private/mission institution; significantly higher than government colleges.
- Review signal: Excellent teaching reputation, good infrastructure, strong clinical environment, very competitive.

Other strong Karnataka options worth considering if you want alternatives:
- Kasturba Medical College (KMC), Manipal
- M.S. Ramaiah Medical College, Bengaluru
- Kasturba Medical College, Mangalore

Short verdict:
- Best value for money: Osmania Medical College / Gandhi Medical College
- Best private option: St. John’s Medical College
- Best overall if considering private reputation in Karnataka: St. John’s, followed by KMC Manipal and MS Ramaiah
