# AI Personal Stylist & Virtual Try-On Platform (POC)

## Objective

Build a complete Proof of Concept (POC) for an AI-powered Personal Stylist and Virtual Try-On platform.

The application should allow users to create a persistent shopping profile, upload body images and measurements, analyze products from any e-commerce website, recommend the best size, summarize customer reviews, and generate virtual try-on images.

The solution should be modular, production-ready, and easily extensible to cloud services later.
