# Culture at Aethel AI

## Vision
To build a future where AI is not just smart, but trustworthy — created through collaborative innovation, ethical rigor, and a culture that empowers every team member.

## Mission
We believe great AI is built by great people. Our culture fosters technical excellence, intellectual curiosity, and a shared commitment to responsible machine learning.

## Core Values
1. **Ethical Innovation** - Prioritize human welfare over technical capability
2. **Global Collaboration** - Leverage distributed expertise across time zones
3. **Transparency** - Maintain open communication about technical decisions
4. **Continuous Learning** - Allocate 20% of engineering hours to skills development

## Work Environment
- **Remote-First**: Hybrid flexibility with 80% remote and 20% collocated
- **Asynchronous Workflows**: Document-driven communication via Notion + async standups
- **Global Talent**: 15+ time zones represented in our engineering teams

## Ethical AI Commitments
- Regular third-party audits for bias detection
- Mandatory ethics training for all ML engineers
- Contribution to AI for Social Good open-source initiatives
- Partnership with Montreal AI Ethics Institute

## Engineering Culture
- **Weekly Hack Nights**: 24-hour sprints with cross-disciplinary teams
- **Code Without Borders**: 30+ open-source projects maintained
- **Innovation Fund**: $500k annual budget for experimental research
- **Peer Review Guild**: Mandatory code reviews with automated quality gates
