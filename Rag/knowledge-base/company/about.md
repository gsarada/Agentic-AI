# About Aethel AI

Aethel AI was founded in 2020 with a mission to redefine artificial intelligence through decentralized innovation and ethical machine learning. Our vision is to create adaptive, trustless AI systems that empower enterprises without compromising privacy or autonomy.

## Core Technology Focus
- **Decentralized Neural Networks:** Federated learning frameworks that enable model training across distributed data sources
- **Enterprise LLM Orchestration:** Proprietary pipeline systems for deploying, monitoring, and fine-tuning large language models at scale
- **Privacy-Preserving AI:** Differential privacy implementations and secure multi-party computation protocols

Our technology stack prioritizes scalability, security, and developer productivity through hybrid cloud/on-prem deployment capabilities.
