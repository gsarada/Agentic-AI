# Career Opportunities at Aethel AI

## Hiring Philosophy
We seek individuals who challenge technical boundaries while upholding ethical standards. Our recruitment process emphasizes:
- **Technical Rigor** - Practical assessments of ML system design and algorithm optimization
- **Cultural Fit** - Demonstrated commitment to collaborative problem-solving
- **Impact Orientation** - Focus on candidates with track records of deploying AI solutions
- **Continuous Growth** - Preference for learners who actively contribute to open-source communities

## Standard Benefits
- **Compensation**: Competitive base salaries with performance-based bonuses (30% above market average for senior roles)
- **Remote Support**: $1,500 annual remote work stipend + ergonomic home office setup
- **Learning Budget**: $10k/year for conferences, certifications, and advanced education
- **Health & Wellness**: Comprehensive medical/dental coverage plus mental healthcare platform access
- **Equity Incentives**: Stock options for full-time employees starting at 1.5% vesting over 4 years

## Career Development Paths
### AI Research
- **Entry** → **Research Scientist** → **Senior Researcher** → **Lead Researcher**
- Focus areas: Decentralized training algorithms, privacy-preserving architectures, quantum machine learning
### Engineering
- **ML Engineer** → **Senior Software Engineer** → **Principal Architect**
- Tracks available: Model deployment, system reliability, enterprise security, and data infrastructure

## Why Join Aethel AI?
- **Pioneering Work**: Help shape AI standards for decentralized financial systems and healthcare diagnostics
- **Ethical Impact**: Your work directly supports UN Sustainable Development Goals through open-source contributions
- **Technical Freedom**: 20% time to explore moonshot projects and prototype novel AI/ML applications
- **Global Community**: Collaborate with research partners in MIT Media Lab, TU Delft, and Stanford HAI
- **Leadership Pipeline**: Rotational programs for high-potential employees to transition into technical management

We offer flexible scheduling, unlimited PTO (with an average team usage of 25+ days/year), and a commitment to promoting from within. Aethel AI invests in your potential as we collectively build the next evolution of responsible artificial intelligence.
