# Contract Reference: AE-CON-2026-004
**Contract Type:** Enterprise Master License Agreement
**Counterparty:** TechCorp
**Effective Date:** 2026-04-01 | **Expiration Date:** 2028-04-01
---
### 1. Executive Summary
Aethel AI will provide TechCorp with a suite of AI-powered tools, licensing 500 seats for a period of two years.
### 2. Financial Terms & Value
* **Total Contract Value (TCV):** $1,000,000
* **Payment Terms:** Net 60
### 3. Key AI/Data Clauses
* **Data Ownership:** TechCorp retains data ownership, with Aethel AI granted permission to use anonymized data for model training.
* **IP Rights:** Aethel AI retains ownership of derivative models and fine-tuned weights.
### 4. Support
* **Onboarding:** Aethel AI will provide onboarding support for a period of 30 days.
* **Technical Support:** Aethel AI will provide technical support via phone and email, with a response time of 1 hour.
### 4. Termination & Liability
In the event of a data breach, Aethel AI shall notify TechCorp within 24 hours and take prompt action to mitigate the breach.
