# Contract Reference: AE-CON-2026-015
**Contract Type:** NDA/Partnership/AI Research Collaboration Agreement
**Counterparty:** AI Research Institute
**Effective Date:** 2026-03-01 | **Expiration Date:** 2028-03-01
---
### 1. Executive Summary
Aethel AI and AI Research Institute will collaborate on AI research projects for a period of two years, with a focus on developing new AI models.
### 2. Financial Terms & Value
* **Total Contract Value (TCV):** $750,000
* **Payment Terms:** Quarterly Installments
### 3. Key AI/Data Clauses
* **Data Ownership:** Both parties retain data ownership, with permission to use anonymized data for model training.
* **IP Rights:** Both parties retain ownership of derivative models and fine-tuned weights, with joint ownership of new IP developed during the collaboration.
### 4. Support
* **Onboarding:** Both parties will provide onboarding support for a period of 90 days.
* **Technical Support:** Both parties will provide technical support via phone and email, with a response time of 2 hours.
### 4. Termination & Liability
In the event of a dispute, both parties shall engage in good-faith negotiations to resolve the dispute.
