# Contract Reference: AE-CON-2026-011
**Contract Type:** Vendor/Cloud Infrastructure Agreement
**Counterparty:** CloudPro
**Effective Date:** 2026-11-01 | **Expiration Date:** 2027-11-01
---
### 1. Executive Summary
Aethel AI will provision compute resources from CloudPro for a period of one year, with a total of 30 GPU clusters.
### 2. Financial Terms & Value
* **Total Contract Value (TCV):** $150,000
* **Payment Terms:** Annual Upfront
### 3. Key AI/Data Clauses
* **Data Ownership:** Aethel AI retains data ownership, with CloudPro granted permission to use anonymized data for internal analytics.
* **IP Rights:** Aethel AI retains ownership of derivative models and fine-tuned weights.
### 4. Support
* **Onboarding:** CloudPro will provide onboarding support for a period of 60 days.
* **Technical Support:** CloudPro will provide technical support via phone and email, with a response time of 2 hours.
### 4. Termination & Liability
In the event of a service outage, CloudPro shall notify Aethel AI within 1 hour and take prompt action to restore services.
